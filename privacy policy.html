Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to Joke Central. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

1. Data Collection

Joke Central is designed to be a "Privacy First" application. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect, store, or sell any of your personal identification information (such as your name, email, or IP address) on our servers.

2. Local Storage

When you use the "Favorites" feature, the data (the text of the joke) is stored exclusively in your browser's Local Storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developers of Joke Central. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache or deleting individual favorites.

3. Third-Party Content

We fetch jokes from external sources:

  • Reddit API: To provide "Dad Jokes."
  • JokeAPI: To provide "Programming" and "Pun" categories.

These third-party services may collect technical data (like your IP address) as part of the standard web request process. Please consult their respective privacy policies for more information.

4. Social Sharing

If you choose to share a joke on X (Twitter), you will be redirected to their platform. We do not track what you share or provide any data to social media platforms beyond the text of the joke you selected.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
